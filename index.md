## 3. Information We Collect

We minimize the data we handle. The categories below describe the **maximum** data the App may
process; several categories apply only if you opt in or only when ads are shown.

### 3.1 Advertising data (Google AdMob)

Unless you have purchased the "Remove Ads" option, the App displays advertisements served by
**Google AdMob** (the Google Mobile Ads SDK). To serve, cap, and measure ads and to prevent fraud,
Google may collect and process:

- a **device advertising identifier** (e.g., the Android Advertising ID);
- approximate, **coarse information** about your device and app (such as device type, operating
  system version, language, and general region inferred from IP address); and
- **ad-interaction data** (such as ad impressions, clicks, and similar diagnostics).

This processing is performed by Google in accordance with Google's own policies. Before ads are
initialized, the App runs Google's **User Messaging Platform (UMP)** consent flow; where required
(for example, in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and Switzerland), you are presented
with a consent choice, and ad personalization depends on your selection. See Section 7 (Advertising
and Your Choices).

### 3.2 Optional "Audience Insights" usage analytics (opt-in; off by default)

The App contains an **optional** feature called **Audience Insights**. It is **disabled by default**
and only operates if **all** of the following are true: (a) you have **expressly opted in**;
(b) Audience Insights is enabled; and (c) you are **not under 18 years of age**. If you do not opt
in, no Audience Insights data is collected, stored for upload, or transmitted.

If you opt in, the App stores, and then uploads on a periodic (approximately daily) basis to our
Google Firebase **Cloud Firestore** database, **anonymous, aggregate-style usage events**, which may
include:

- a randomly generated **installation identifier** ("install ID") — a random UUID created on your
  device. This identifier is **not** your device's advertising ID, **not** a hardware identifier,
  and is **not** linked to your name or any account. It exists solely to de-duplicate events from
  the same installation;
- the **type of event** from a fixed, limited list (for example: session start/end, screen viewed,
  a hand dealt, a hand result, an in-game action such as hit/stand/double/split, counting system
  selected, training screen used, music control used, a settings change, or an error);
- **coarse, non-identifying technical context**: the App version, the Android OS version, a broad
  device category (phone or tablet), a broad screen-size bucket, your device language, and a broad
  **region** value (only if you provided one in the optional in-app survey described in Section 3.3).

We do **not** use Firebase Analytics. Audience Insights data is written only to Cloud Firestore,
scoped to the anonymous install ID, and is used to understand general, aggregate usage trends so we
can improve the App.

You can turn this feature off at any time and delete the locally stored Audience Insights data from
within the App (see Section 9).

### 3.3 Optional in-app survey

The App may offer an **optional** survey (for example, asking about your experience level, your
reason for using the App, a training priority, an age range, and a general region). Answering is
voluntary. Survey responses are stored **on your device**. Your **general region** and your
**age range** may influence the optional Audience Insights feature: the region value may be included
in Audience Insights events (only if you opted in), and an age range indicating you are under 18
**disables** Audience Insights entirely.

### 3.4 In-app purchases (Google Play Billing)

The App offers optional one-time in-app purchases (for example, "Remove Ads," practice-chip packs,
table themes, and card skins). All purchases are processed by **Google Play Billing**. We do **not**
receive or store your payment card number or other payment credentials. The App stores only a local
record of your entitlements (for example, a boolean indicating that ads are removed, and the set of
owned non-consumable items) so that your purchases function and can be restored.

### 3.5 Data stored only on your device

The following data is stored locally on your device (using Android DataStore) and is **not**
transmitted to us, except where expressly described above:

- your simulated chip balance (bankroll) and current game/session state;
- your game and audio settings (such as deck count, payout rules, sound and music preferences);
- your training progress and selected counting system;
- your purchase entitlements;
- the optional install ID and optional survey responses described above.

This local data remains on your device until you delete it within the App, clear the App's storage,
or uninstall the App.

### 3.6 Information we do NOT collect

We do **not** request, collect, or store: your name, email address, phone number, postal address,
contacts, calendar, photos or media library, files, precise/GPS location, camera images, or
microphone audio. The App does not require you to create an account and does not collect login
credentials.

## 10. Data Retention

- **Local game data and settings** are retained on your device until you delete them, clear the
  App's storage, or uninstall the App.
- **Pending Audience Insights events** awaiting upload are retried a limited number of times within
  an approximately **48-hour** window; events that cannot be uploaded in that window are
  automatically **pruned** from your device. Events that upload successfully are deleted from your
  device immediately after upload.
- **Uploaded Audience Insights events** stored in our Cloud Firestore database are retained only as
  long as reasonably necessary for the aggregate-analytics purposes described in this Policy, after
  which they are deleted or further aggregated.
- Data processed by Google for advertising and purchases is retained by Google according to Google's
  policies.
